AIM (Artificial Intelligence for Musicians) is a Purdue music technology research group, whose aim is to create reactive, human-like systems which support musicians during their practice sessions and performances.

Some of AIM's projects are supported by a [National Science Foundation grant](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/show-award/?AWD_ID=2326198).

We are looking for motivated graduate or undergraduate students to join our team. Click [here](https://engineering.purdue.edu/VIP/teams/tagged_items/aim) for info.
