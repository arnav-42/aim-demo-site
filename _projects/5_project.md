Spring 2024 - present

As the name suggests, Robot Cello is a project about using reinforcement
learning to teach a robot arm to play cello. The project is currently in its
survey phase but is investigating using motion capture technology to get
training data for an RL model.

We partner with the [Purdue Envision Center](https://www.rcac.purdue.edu/envision)
to collect motion data for our robot arm to train on. On the left is a video of
Prof. Yun playing cello while wearing a motion-capture rig. Our demo:
[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rf0bSJr8yFM](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rf0bSJr8yFM)
