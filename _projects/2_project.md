Fall 2023 - present

Evaluator is an app that aims to help musicians practice more effectively. It
utilizes computer vision and YOLO localization techniques to help musicians
track, analyze, and improve their posture.

It also uses spectrogram analysis and multimodal transformers to help musicians
identify their mistakes in music and correct them.
