Spring 2022 - present

Mus2Vid is a real-time art project that uses diffusion models to generate video
depictions in response to classical music. It uses recurrent and transformer
networks to analyze input audio and estimate its emotion and genre qualities,
which are converted into text and fed to a text-to-image diffusion model to
generate images.

## Visualize Music for Interactive Entertainment

### Introduction

Music is a powerful and universal language that has the unique ability to unite
people from diverse backgrounds and cultures. Yet, for those who are
hearing-impaired, this enchanting language remains largely inaccessible. Our
ambitious project seeks to break down these barriers by embarking on a mission
to visualize music, making it not only accessible but also a source of
immersive and interactive entertainment for everyone, regardless of their
hearing abilities.

### Statement of Problem

Imagine one out of every five friends in your life is unable to share the joy
of music with you due to hearing impairment. When you get swept away by the
rhythm, or experience laughter and tears stirred by the emotions within a song,
they can only observe in silence, unable to partake in the world of music that
engulfs you.

These friends feel a profound sense of regret, unable to relish the uplifting
and emotionally resonant experiences that music has to offer. They miss out on
the exquisite melodies, harmonies, and the enchanting allure of music, unable
to share in the same joy and emotional connection that music provides to
others. This sorrow persists throughout their lives, for music is a captivating
art form, and they are unable to fully immerse themselves in its splendor.

However, this inequity is unjust, as everyone has the right to savor music, a
universal gift and a testament to the cultural evolution of humanity. Nature
may have deprived them of the ability to hear music, but we aspire to employ
modern technology to restore to them the enjoyment they rightfully deserve.

### Proposed Solution

Our project aims to develop innovative technologies that transform music into a
visual and interactive experience. We focus on using emotions to create
connections between different senses and by aligning emotions to ensure the
relevance of music, words, and pictures to achieve the visualization of music.

We are committed to harnessing the power of cutting-edge technology to bridge
the gap between auditory and visual senses, immersing the audience in a
multisensory journey beyond the traditional music experience. Our approach
revolves around the harmonious fusion of music, text, and visuals, with
emotions as the common thread weaving this intricate tapestry.

#### Music Analysis: Multidimensional Exploration

Our journey begins with a deep analysis of music, dissecting it from various
angles. We examine instruments, capturing the complexity of the overall sonic
landscape contributed by each instrument. Emotions become a pivotal element
when delving into the feelings and moods conveyed by the composition. Elements
such as rhythm, pitch range, harmony, and dynamics are meticulously studied to
understand the subtle nuances of the musical work.

#### Machine Learning Classification: Understanding Emotional Resonance

To truly grasp the emotional essence of musical compositions, we employ
state-of-the-art machine learning models. These models are designed to classify
music and its associated emotions based on textual descriptions. This process
bridges the gap between abstract emotional realms and the digital domain,
allowing us to establish tangible connections between music and the visual
experiences it evokes.

#### Text Prompts: Adding Text to Music

Once machine learning models complete their work, they generate descriptive
text prompts that encapsulate the emotional core of the music. These prompts
act as keys to unlocking the visual representation of auditory masterpieces.
Through the power of language, our aim is to provide the audience with rich
context for understanding and appreciating music.

#### Generation Models: Visualizing Music

These text prompts form the foundation of our generation models. These models
transform abstract descriptions into vivid, interactive visual effects. From
evocative images to mesmerizing videos, generation models breathe life into
music, offering the audience an entirely new dimension of experience. Each
visual element is meticulously crafted to complement the music, creating an
enchanting sensory symphony.

#### Continuous Updates: Real-time Personalization

Our innovation doesn't stop at static visual effects; it evolves in real time
as the music plays. Visual effects harmonize with the ebb and flow of the
music, ensuring that each audience member receives a personalized and
interactive entertainment experience. This dynamic feature adds an element of
surprise, making every encounter with music a unique and captivating journey.

### Impact

Our project has the potential to bring about significant changes in the way
people experience and appreciate music. It strives to achieve the following
impacts:

- Accessibility for hearing-impaired individuals
- Personalized visual entertainment
- Enhancing the entertainment industry
- Expanding horizons for music enthusiasts
- Inclusivity and innovation through machine learning and generation models

In summary, our project seeks to impact not only the lives of those with
hearing impairments but also the entire music ecosystem. By providing novel
ways to experience music and entertainment, we hope to create a positive ripple
effect that enhances the industry and enriches the lives of music enthusiasts.
